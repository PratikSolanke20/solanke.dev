Clinical Overview

Acne & Pimples

(Yauvanapidaka)

A deep integration of ancient Ayurvedic pathology and modern dermatological science to understand and eradicate chronic breakouts from the root.

Pitta Aggravation

Inflammatory Acne

Excess heat (Ushna) in the body manifests as painful, red, inflamed papules and pustules. The blood (Rakta Dhatu) becomes toxic due to spicy diets, extreme stress, and hormonal fluctuations.

Triggers: Spicy foods, fermented foods, summer heat, stress.

Kapha Aggravation

Cystic Acne & Comedones

Excess earth and water elements cause overproduction of sebum (oil). This mixes with dead skin cells to cause deep, hard cystic lesions, blackheads, and whiteheads that are slow to heal.

Triggers: Dairy, excessive sweets, fried foods, lethargic lifestyle.

Ayurvedic Treatment Protocol

Rakta Shodhana

Blood purification using internal herbs like Neem and Manjistha to remove deep-seated toxins.

Lepa Application

External application of cooling, anti-inflammatory pastes made from sandalwood and turmeric.

Ahara Vichara

Dietary modifications focusing on cooling, bitter, and astringent tastes to pacify Pitta and Kapha.
