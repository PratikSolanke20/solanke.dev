The Perfect Morning Routine for Pitta Skin

Calming the fire before the day begins.

If you wake up with redness, sensitivity, or new inflammatory breakouts, your skin is likely Pitta-dominant. Pitta represents the elements of Fire and Water, and its primary characteristic is heat. Starting your day correctly is crucial.

1. Skip the Harsh Cleanser

Most foaming cleansers strip the skin's acid mantle, triggering your skin to overproduce oil. Instead, wash your face with cool (never hot) water. Use a gentle chickpea flour (Besan) paste.

2. Floral Hydration

Mist your face generously with pure, steam-distilled Rose Water (Gulab Jal). Rose is extremely cooling (Sheetala) and acts as a natural astringent to tighten pores without alcohol.

3. Aloe & Sandalwood

For moisture, avoid heavy creams. Use pure Aloe Vera gel mixed with a tiny pinch of white sandalwood powder. This acts as a protective, cooling barrier against daytime heat.
